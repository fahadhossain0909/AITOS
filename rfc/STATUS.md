# RFC Status Definitions

Version: 1.0

---

## Draft

Initial proposal.

Author is still refining the document.

---

## Discussion

Feedback is actively being collected.

No implementation should begin.

---

## Under Review

Formal engineering review.

Architecture and standards compliance are evaluated.

---

## Approved

Proposal accepted.

Implementation may begin.

---

## Implemented

Proposal has been successfully implemented.

Associated ADR should exist.

---

## Rejected

Proposal declined.

Reason should be documented.

---

## Withdrawn

Proposal voluntarily closed by the author.

---

# Status Rules

Only Approved RFCs may proceed to implementation.

Rejected RFCs remain part of project history.

RFC history must never be deleted.
