# AES-002 — Architecture Standard

## Objective

Architecture must remain modular, scalable, and technology-independent.

---

## Principles

- Modular Design
- Stable Interfaces
- Low Coupling
- High Cohesion
- Replaceable Components

---

## Requirements

Architecture must define:

- Components
- Interfaces
- Responsibilities
- Dependencies
- Data Flow
- Failure Modes

---

## Decision Recording

Major architectural decisions require an ADR.

Breaking changes require an RFC before implementation.
