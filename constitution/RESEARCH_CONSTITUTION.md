# Research Constitution

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Research is the foundation of AITOS.

Engineering decisions should originate from validated research rather than assumptions.

---

# Research Lifecycle

Idea

↓

Hypothesis

↓

Evidence Collection

↓

Experiment

↓

Validation

↓

Documentation

↓

Knowledge

↓

Implementation

---

# Research Principles

## Evidence First

Claims require supporting evidence.

---

## Reproducibility

Experiments should be repeatable.

---

## Documentation

Research must be documented before implementation.

---

## Traceability

Every important conclusion should reference its supporting evidence.

---

## Continuous Learning

Negative results are valuable.

Failed experiments improve future decision making.

---

# Research Standards

Research should define:

- Objective
- Scope
- Assumptions
- Methodology
- Results
- Limitations
- Future Work

---

# Constitutional Rule

Implementation without research should be considered an exception, not the default.
