# Engineering Constitution

## Engineering Philosophy

Engineering decisions must prioritize long-term maintainability over short-term convenience.

---

## Mandatory Rules

- Every feature requires documentation.
- Every architectural change requires review.
- Every major decision should be recorded in an ADR.
- Every significant proposal should begin as an RFC.
- Every production change should be traceable.

---

## Engineering Priorities

1. Correctness
2. Simplicity
3. Readability
4. Reliability
5. Performance
6. Scalability

Performance optimization must never compromise correctness.
