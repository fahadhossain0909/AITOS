# ADR Status Definitions

Version: 1.0

---

## Proposed

The decision has been drafted but not yet reviewed.

Implementation must not begin.

---

## Under Review

Architecture review is in progress.

Feedback is being collected.

---

## Accepted

The decision has been approved.

Implementation may begin.

---

## Implemented

The architecture has been implemented successfully.

---

## Deprecated

The decision is no longer recommended.

It remains documented for historical purposes.

---

## Superseded

The ADR has been replaced by a newer ADR.

The newer ADR should be referenced explicitly.

---

# Status Rules

Only Accepted ADRs should guide future implementation.

Deprecated and Superseded ADRs remain immutable historical records.

---

# Example

ADR-0003

Status: Superseded

Superseded By:

ADR-0011
