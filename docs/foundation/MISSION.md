# AITOS Mission

The mission of AITOS is to create a production-grade engineering platform for financial intelligence.

The platform will:

- Support systematic market research.
- Preserve institutional knowledge.
- Assist human decision making.
- Reduce operational risk.
- Enable AI-assisted engineering.
- Encourage modular and maintainable software architecture.

Success is measured by the quality of decisions, documentation, research, and engineering—not by short-term trading profits.
