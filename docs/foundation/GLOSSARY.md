# Glossary

This glossary defines the terminology used throughout the AITOS project.

---

## AITOS

Adaptive Intelligence Trading & Research Operating System.

---

## Evidence

Verified information used to support engineering or trading decisions.

---

## Knowledge

Validated information derived from evidence and research.

---

## Research

A structured process of investigating, validating, and documenting ideas.

---

## Architecture

The structural design of the platform, including components, interfaces, and interactions.

---

## Decision Engine

The subsystem responsible for generating explainable decision recommendations.

---

## Knowledge OS

The subsystem responsible for storing, organizing, and retrieving validated knowledge.

---

## Research OS

The subsystem responsible for managing experiments, observations, and evidence.

---

## Data OS

The subsystem responsible for collecting, validating, and preparing financial data.

---

## Intelligence OS

The subsystem responsible for AI reasoning, analysis, and decision support.

---

## Execution OS

The subsystem responsible for paper trading and live execution workflows.

---

## AI Context

Structured documentation that enables AI coding assistants to understand project requirements, architecture, and standards.

---

## ADR

Architecture Decision Record.

A permanent record explaining why an architectural decision was made.

---

## RFC

Request for Comments.

A proposal describing significant changes before implementation.

---

## Paper Trading

Executing strategies in a simulated environment without risking real capital.

---

## Production

The environment in which validated components operate with real users or capital.
