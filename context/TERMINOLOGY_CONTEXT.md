# Terminology Context

Purpose

Provide a single vocabulary for humans and AI.

---

## Rule

Every important term should have exactly one canonical definition.

Avoid synonyms in architecture documents.

---

## Examples

Signal

A machine-generated observation suggesting a potential market opportunity.

---

Decision

A validated action selected after evaluating evidence.

---

Evidence

Verified information supporting a conclusion.

---

Experiment

A repeatable process used to validate a hypothesis.

---

Knowledge

Validated information derived from documented evidence.

---

Pattern

A reusable solution that solves recurring problems.

---

Module

An independently maintainable subsystem.

---

Interface

A stable communication boundary between modules.
